Lord of the Rings

One Ring to Rule Them All

Facts

  • The Lord of the Rings is a film series directed by Peter Jackson.
  • The series is based on the novel written by J.R.R. Tolkien.
  • The main characters include Frodo Baggins, Aragorn, Gandalf, and Legolas.

More Information

  • The series consists of three movies: The Fellowship of the Ring, The Two Towers, and The Return of the King.
  • The movies were released between 2001 and 2003.
  • The series won numerous awards, including multiple Academy Awards.
